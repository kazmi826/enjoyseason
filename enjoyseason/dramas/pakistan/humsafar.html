About Humsafar:
Humsafar is a Pakistani romantic drama television serial that aired on Hum TV from October 2011 to January 2012. It is based on the novel of the same name by Farhat Ishtiaq. The drama stars Fawad Khan and Mahira Khan and is considered one of the greatest Pakistani dramas ever made.
