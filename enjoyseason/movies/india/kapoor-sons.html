About Kapoor and Sons:
Kapoor and Sons is a 2016 Indian Hindi-language drama film directed by Shakun Batra. The film stars Rishi Kapoor, Rajat Kapoor, Ratna Pathak Shah, Alia Bhatt, Sidharth Malhotra, and Fawad Khan. It was released on 18 March 2016 and received critical acclaim.
